Successes

We are extremely proud to announce that as of July 15, 2017, we have helped over 80,000 children fight sickness and injuries. Because of this, president Barack Obama awarded us the Drucker Prize for our innovative ways for saving children. Recently, we have begun to distribute limited, low-price healthcare.
